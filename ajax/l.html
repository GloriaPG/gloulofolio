Lead Systems

Family company with two core businesses: the bakery and supermarket. My role as systems leader, consisted of the following activities:

  • POS Support, system made in FoxPro.
  • Maintaining the server.
  • Preventive, corrective and predictive maintenance.
  • User support.
  • Accounting.
  • Administration.

My accomplishments within the company were:
Implementation of a sales point at the bakery, using ASPEL CAJA,automation with ASPEL SAE and PROD,staff training,integration of staff, reducing costs by automating the recording of inputs and outputs of raw materials.
